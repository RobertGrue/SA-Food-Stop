Events

Welcome to the events page! This page updates weekly to keep you up to date with all the fun events in San Antonio.

  • 31
    October

    San Antonio Zoo 3903 N. St. Mary's St. San Antonio

    Enjoy FREE trick-or-treating, pumpkin spice, and everything nice at San Antonio Zoo during the Halloween season for Zoo Boo!, presented by PNC Bank. This family-friendly, non-scary Halloween celebration opens daily from September 21 to October 31, inviting visitors to enjoy a varied lineup of activities and attractions, including daily FREE trick-or-treating. This year, visitors can also look forward to encountering GIANT animatronic bugs, offering an exciting chance to come face to face with larger-than-life moving creatures like bees, butterflies, spiders, dung beetles, praying mantises, and scorpions. These animatronic marvels provide perfect photo opportunities as you trick or treat, adding another layer of fun to the festivities.

    Time:9:00am

  • 14
    October

    Broadway-1603

    Get ready for an unforgettable fall experience at the San Antonio Pumpkin Festival, where the charm of the season meets the vibrant energy of the city! From September 27 to October 27, join us at 1603 Broadway for a magical journey featuring a nostalgic train ride through a replica Alamo, a whimsical carousel, and a massive corn pit that's more fun than a day at the beach. Explore our Wild West Village, bounce around on our giant pumpkins, and snap Insta-worthy photos with over 45 unique pumpkin varieties.

    Price: $15

    Time:10:00am to 10:00pm

  • 18
    October

    950 E. Grayson St. San Antonio, TX

    Join us for an evening of sophistication at Lambermont Events on Friday, October 18, 2024. Unlock the secrets of Edgar Allan Poe, our inspiration for this event, and what lies beneath the floorboards. As you wander through the evening of live performances and crafted cocktails, you'll find yourself surrounded by the beauty of his works and this historic home. If you snag a VIP ticket, you'll get special access to the opulent first-floor Speakeasy. Garden guests will join fellow literary enthusiasts under the moonlight for more entertainment and creative conversation. Dress to impress in your best Victorian attire and prepare for a night filled with laughter, intrigue and good company! Secure your tickets now- Prices will increase as the event date approaches.

    Price:$35 Evening Garden Pass $135 VIP Hidden Parlor Pass

    Time: 7:30pm to 11:30pm

    This is not a haunted house event.
